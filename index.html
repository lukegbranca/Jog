FAQ

Questions? We've got you.

Can't find the answer? Give us a call — we're always happy to chat.

Call Us Now
How do I get started?

Simply give us a call on 0408 571 813. We'll organise a free meet and greet at your home so your dog can meet their new walker before the first session.

Are you insured and police checked?

Yes! All staff are police checked, first-aid trained, and we carry full public liability insurance. Your dog's safety is our highest priority.

How many dogs walk together?

We keep groups to a maximum of 6 dogs, carefully matched by size and temperament. Dogs who don't suit groups are placed in our private walk service instead.

What areas do you service?

We service Melbourne's eastern suburbs including Ringwood, Croydon, Mitcham, Bayswater, Mooroolbark, Kilsyth and surrounds. Not sure? Give us a call.

Will I get updates during the walk?

Absolutely! We send photos and a GPS-tracked walk report to your phone after every session so you can see exactly where they went and how much fun they had.

My dog is anxious — is that okay?

Of course! We work with all temperaments. We might start with a private walk so your dog can build confidence at their own pace. Our team is patient and experienced.
